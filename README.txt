audio_hdmi_hd3000
============
OS X Sandy Bridge/6 Series/Socket 1155 - HD4000/AMD/Nvidia HDMI Audio
With or Without DSDT Edits

v3: 8/10/2015 - New ssdt method/credit RehabMan, initial 10.11, native CPU/GPU PM
    and Nvidia Maxwell support
v2: 8/15/2014

HDMI audio for OS X HD3000 systems with either: 1. HDMI audio edited dsdt or 2. the HDMI audio ssdt. Both techniques enable native Ivy Bridge graphics power management.
